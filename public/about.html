About MedHive

MedHive is an AI-powered healthcare platform designed specifically for Sri Lanka. We are on a mission to transform how patients, doctors, clinics, and pharmacies interact with medical information.

Our Mission

To unify healthcare in Sri Lanka through intelligent technology. We believe every patient deserves seamless access to their complete medical history, and every healthcare provider deserves the tools to deliver better, more informed care.

What We Do

MedHive provides a comprehensive digital health ecosystem that connects patients, clinics, pharmacies, and healthcare providers through a single, secure platform.

AI Prescription Reader

Digitize handwritten prescriptions using advanced OCR technology, reducing errors and improving pharmacy efficiency.

Unified Med ID

One identifier connects all your medical records across clinics, enabling continuous and informed care.

Lab Report Analysis

Automatically extract and track lab values over time, giving patients and doctors valuable health insights.

Clinic Integration

Seamless connection between doctors and in-house pharmacies for faster, safer prescription fulfillment.

Our Values

Patient First

Every decision we make prioritizes patient safety, privacy, and convenience.

Transparency

We believe in clear, honest communication about how we handle health data.

Innovation

We leverage cutting-edge AI to solve real healthcare challenges in Sri Lanka.

Accessibility

Healthcare technology should be available to everyone, regardless of location or background.

Medical Disclaimer: MedHive is a health information technology platform. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical decisions. Our AI tools assist in digitizing and organizing health information but do not replace professional medical judgment.
